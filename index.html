Pokémon Information

Shaymin

While it is not known to evolve into
or from another Pokémon, Shaymin has a second form
activated by using a Gracidea flower in the daytime.

Sylveon

Sylveon is a quadrupedal, mammalian Pokémon
covered with pale cream-colored fur with pink
feet, ears, and tail.

Jigglypuff

Jigglypuff is perhaps most famous for the song.
it sings which puts everyone who hears
it into a deep sleep
